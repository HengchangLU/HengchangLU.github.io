This project aggregates worldwide datasets covering earthquakes, transportation nodes, energy infrastructure, and national GDP metrics. The goal is to build an interactive map that visualizes these data layers together.

Interactive World Map

The first layer of our map includes data from tsunamis and earthquakes, including the date, magnitude, depth, and location.

Upcoming milestone: integrate transportation, energy, and GDP related data. It will also provide a better measure to differentiate between earthquakes and tsunamis.
